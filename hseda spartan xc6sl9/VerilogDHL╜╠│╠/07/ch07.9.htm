7.9 I mplicit net declarations

Including a previously unused identifier in a terminal list shall implicitly declare a new net of type wire , or of type defined by the compiler directive `default_nettype (see ), with zero delay.

Each implicitly declared net shall connect to one or more of the following:

  • gate output
  • bidirectional terminal
  • module output port
