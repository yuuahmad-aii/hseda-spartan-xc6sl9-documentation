7.8 P ullup and p ulldown sources

The instance declaration of a pullup or a pulldown source shall begin with one of the following keywords: 

 
pullup
							pulldown

A pullup source shall place a logic value 1 on the nets connected in its terminal list. A pulldown source shall place a logic value 0 on the nets connected in its terminal list. The signals that these sources place on nets shall have pull strength in the absence of a strength specification. There shall be no delay specifications for these sources.

The following example declares two pullup instances:

 
pullup
 (strong1)
 p1 (neta), p2 (netb);

In this example, p1 instance drives neta and the other p2 instance drives netb .
