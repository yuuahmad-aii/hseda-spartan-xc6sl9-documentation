5.5 Race conditions

Because the execution of expression evaluation and net update events may be intermingled, race conditions are possible:

 
assign
 p = q; initial
 begin
	q = 1;	#1 q = 0; 	$display
(p); end

The simulator is correct in displaying either a 1 or a 0 here. The assignment of 0 to q enables an update event for p . The simulator may either continue and execute the $display task, or execute the update for p , followed by the $display task.
